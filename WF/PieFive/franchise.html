Qualifications

Are you ready to take your slice of the pie?

Here's what you will need:

• Total commitment to the development of the Pie Five brand

• From $600,000 to $800,000 in liquid assets for five location deals

• Operational experience (restaurant, business, franchise)

• Real estate experience in the market

• Infrastructure and resources to meet our development schedule

 

Initial Investment: Between $344,000 and $483,000

Franchise Fee: $20,000 per location

Development Fee: $5,000 per location

Royalty Rate: 6%

Brand Fund Contribution: 2%

Local Advertising: 3%

Franchise Term: 10 years

(followed by the option of 10 years + 2 five year renewals)

 

This website and the franchise sales information on this site do not constitute an offer to sell a franchise. The offer of a franchise can only be made through the delivery of a franchise disclosure document (FDD) in compliance with the Federal Trade Commission’s rule of franchising and applicable state franchise registration/disclosure laws.

What to Expect

Here are the steps to owning your own franchise

1. Apply: Submit an application and consent form. We will then send you a Franchise Disclosure Document (FDD) to review.

 

2. Meet & greet: After receiving your application and consent form, we will schedule an interview to talk through the details.

 

3. Business plan: Submit your business plan.

 

4. Approval: Once you’ve met all necessary requirements, we will welcome you to the Pie Five family.

 

5. Build your restaurant: Cutting edge mapping and site selection resources will help you identify the best location, and we will help you select contractors from respected local and national firms to construct your restaurant.

 

6. Open your restaurant: We will assist you with training new staff and developing marketing strategies.

 

7. Grand opening and beyond: After helping you plan and execute the grand opening of your new restaurant, we will continue to support your restaurant’s growth through the implementation of marketing programs and ongoing product development.
